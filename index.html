Hello! This is a demonstration of local translation in CKEditor5 using transformers.js.

Select some text, choose a target language from the toolbar, and click the Translate button. The translation happens entirely in your browser using WebGPU or WebAssembly.

Try translating between:

  • Finnish ↔ English
  • Finnish ↔ Swedish
  • English ↔ Swedish

Translation models will be loaded on first use, which may take a few minutes depending on your connection and computer.
